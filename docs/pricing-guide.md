### How to Create a Free Account

1. Go to **[https://cloud.rockrobotic.com/](https://cloud.rockrobotic.com/)**
2. Click **"Create an account"** or **"Sign Up"**
3. **IMPORTANT:** When prompted to select an account type, choose **"Gaussian Splat"** 
   - This creates a SplatLabs account (not a LiDAR/ROCK Cloud account)
4. Complete the registration with email and password
5. The customer now has a **Free Plan** with 2 projects and AI features

---

### How to Upgrade to a Paid Plan

Once a customer has created their free account, here's how they upgrade:

1. **Log in** to their account at [https://cloud.rockrobotic.com/](https://cloud.rockrobotic.com/)
2. Click on their **profile icon** (person icon in the upper right-hand corner)
3. This opens their **Account Page**
4. Click on **"Manage Subscriptions"**
5. Select the desired plan (Starter, Business, or Enterprise)
6. Choose monthly or annual billing
7. Enter payment information and complete the upgrade

**Pro Tip:** Remind customers that annual billing saves 15-17% and Business annual plans include +10 bonus AI projects.
